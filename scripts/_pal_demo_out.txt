USER           | A town of 1200 people grows by 8 percent every year.
               | How many people are there after 10 years, rounded to
               | the nearest whole person?

# Initialize variables
initial_population = 1200
growth_rate = 8 / 100  # Convert percentage to decimal
years = 10
# Calculate population after 10 years using compound interest formula
answer = initial_population * (1 + growth_rate) ** years
# Round the result to the nearest whole person
answer = round(answer)
# Print the final answer
print(answer)
